# KayıPet
KayıPet is a dedicated platform designed to help you find your lost pet or offer a loving home to abandoned animals. With KayıPet, you can easily post advertisements for lost and found pets, connecting caring individuals with furry friends in need. Whether you're searching for your beloved companion or looking to provide a warm home for a pet in need, KayıPet is here to assist you every step of the way.
## 📚 Key Technologies and Libraries
- Kotlin
- MVVM & Clean Architecture
- Mapbox
- Hilt for Dependency Injection
- Coroutines
- Firebase (Authentication, Realtime, Storage)
- Flow
- Navigation
- XML  
- Serialization
